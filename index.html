PsychoTherapeia is Oded Reitten’s London based counselling and psychotherapy practice. The practice operates in Lower-Clapton E5 and in Dalston N8.

Psychotherapy originated from the earlier term, psychotherapeia, combining the meanings of psyche (soul/mind) and therapeia (attending to, caring for). It was used to characterise the “prevention and remedy of disease by psychical influence” (Dendy, 1853) and give prominence to the helpful influence of witnessing, discussing and being with the reality of the other.

Since 2006 I have worked in mental health settings both with individuals and with groups, and facilitated Buddhist meditation workshops. My places of work have included the Terrance-Higgins Trust, REST project at Mind in Camden, London Metropolitan University, the Centre for Better Health, the JBCS bereavement service, and Enosh, the Israeli mental health association.

I currently see clients for individual psychotherapy at my home practice in Lower-Clapton and at my office practice in Dalston. I also work part-time providing therapy to children and adolescence at King Alfred School in Hampstead.

Oded

After initially studying Psychology and Sociology in Israel, I was inspired to search for a wider understanding of life and the human mind. My early counselling training was ‘integrative’, meaning that I engaged with many of the core traditions of psychotherapy. Following on from this, I trained as an existential psychotherapist and a group facilitator.

Since 2006 I have worked in mental health settings both with individuals and with groups, and facilitated Buddhist meditation sessions. My places of work have included London Metropolitan University, the Centre for Better Health, the JBCS bereavement service, and Enosh, the Israeli mental health association.

I currently see clients for individual therapy at my private practice, at the Terrance Higgins Trust and at King Alfred School. I also work as a facilitator for a support group at Mind in Camden for the REST project, which supports people who experience dependency of minor tranquillisers.

Sessions & fees

I charge £65 per session. I keep a few concession slots for clients who cannot afford the full fee. Please inquire for availability.

Sessions last fifty minutes and are a weekly or fortnightly commitment. If you miss a session I will still need to charge you for it unless you canceled the session more then 48 hours in advanced. All sessions and records of sessions are completely confidential. In case of a serious risk to self or others, confidentiality may be reviewed, but always with full consultation between myself and the client.

Contact

If you are interested in finding out more about my practice, or would like to make an appointment, you are welcome to contact me via email, telephone or text message. Once you get in touch, I will invite you for an initial session to explore your need for psychotherapy. This session will allow us to see whether we can work together and to decide the length and conditions of our work.
